SAAi Lite Privacy Policy

SAAi does not collect, store, or transmit any user data whatsoever. As a content viewer, it offers no facility for the user to enter and record any personally identifiable information.

Apple will hold information on you as part of your transaction with them when downloading this app from the App Store. For more information, see the Apple Privacy Policy.
